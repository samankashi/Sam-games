Privacy Policy

This Speed Test app is committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information Collection

We collect network-related data (IP address, signal strength, network type) and device information to perform accurate speed tests and provide device details.

2. Location Data

Location permission is required to identify nearby test servers and to access cellular network technical details as per Android security requirements.

3. Data Usage

Your test results are stored locally on your device in the 'History' section. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google AdMob for advertisements, which may collect device identifiers to serve personalized ads.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

By using this app, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.
