Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 06, 2026

Welcome to Phone Tester. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in the Phone Tester application. By using the app, you agree to these terms.

2. Permissions and Data Access

Phone Tester requires several device permissions for hardware diagnostics only:

  • CAMERA: To test camera functionality and flashlight.
  • MICROPHONE: To test audio recording levels.
  • SENSORS: To verify hardware sensor operations.
  • VIBRATE: To test the haptic motor.
  • WIFI/BLUETOOTH: To verify connectivity.
  • STORAGE: To export test reports as PDF files.

3. Data Collection

We do NOT collect or store any personal user data. Hardware test results are processed and stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

4. Advertising and Analytics

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. To provide relevant ads, AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID (Android 13+ compliant). You can reset this ID in your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google Play Services for advertisements and core functions. These third parties may collect information as per their own privacy policies.

6. Security

We ensure that no sensitive data leaves your device. We follow industry standards to protect all technical info processed during tests.

7. Contact Us

For any questions regarding privacy, please contact us via the developer contact information on the Google Play Store.
